Opportunity Solution Tree

Session → FBRS = 0.52%

Owner: Nils Alm · Target: 2026-06-30 · Product Team KR, Q2 2026 · Status: Active, at risk · Refreshed 2026-06-11
Outcome
Lift session to first-booking-request-sent conversion from ~0.43% to 0.52% by 2026-06-30.
The funnel decomposes into three multiplicative gaps; each gap is a top-level branch of this tree. Work started in the booking flow where clarity was highest. Branches 1 and 2 are the next discovery investments.
Sessions → VDP (~48%) × VDP → ATC (~5%) × ATC → FBRS (~17% first-timer / ~25% overall) ≈ ~0.5%
Branch 1: Vehicle discovery Branch 2: Vehicle selection Branch 3: Booking flow No project yet Biggest project-less lever
Branch 1

Vehicle discovery

Session → VDP
Sustain ~48%; lift VDP→ATC indirectly via better intent
Opp 1.1

Search does not reveal what a vehicle has

"I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need"
  • Search filters: add-on, equipment, rules filters
  • MC-1567: default sort = distance for location searches
Opp 1.2

Pickup location matters more than the list shows

"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"
  • Search map: map-first results
Opp 1.3

Listings blur together

"The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out"
  • Vehicle personalisation / name: owner-named vehicles for character plus a marketplace-vitality loop
Opp 1.4 No project yet

Post-decline recovery

"My request got declined and now I have to start over"
Declines drop renters into a generic search with no preserved context. Intent-preservation problem shared with 1.5 and 2.4. Candidate for triage.
Opp 1.5 No project yet

Resume search for returning renters

"I came back to keep looking and lost my place"
Guest state is not persisted; even logged-in users start over. Likely a meaningful slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions. Candidate for triage.
Opp 1.6 No project yet

Favorites are hidden after an unsuccessful request

"I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked"
Favorites/wishlist is hidden and not very accessible; making it more prominent could recover this intent.
Opp 1.7 No project yet

Multiple favorite lists per trip

"I'm planning different trips and I want to save vehicles to different favorite lists to keep my search focused"
Vehicles differ in availability, fit, and location, so different trips need different lists.
Branch 2

Vehicle selection

VDP → ATC
+1 to 2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)
Opp 2.1

Booking is a leap into the unknown

"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"
  • Timeline for booking on VDP: surfaces the 6-step booking journey; A/B 50/50, 4 weeks
Opp 2.2

No social proof on owner or vehicle

"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"
  • Rich reviews: renter context, trip context, sub-ratings, photos
  • Social proof: click-percentile signals, "recently booked", calendar freshness
Opp 2.3

Total price invisible on mobile

"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"
  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile: sticky footer with total price plus cost overlay
Opp 2.4 Biggest lever

Non-fitting landing with nowhere to go

"This vehicle isn't quite it, and I don't see anywhere to go from here"
  • Vehicle recommendations on VDP
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
  • AI assistant to suggest three options, getting feedback
~102k sessions per 2 months land on a non-matching vehicle and bounce. Sized at +65 to +180 FBRS/month, 50 to 140% of the KR gap. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.4 and 1.5.
Opp 2.5 No project yet

Vehicle fit information is thin

"Is this vehicle the right one?"
More information on what the vehicle entails: bedding setup, clearer add-on communication, better gallery, gas consumption and carbon footprint.
Opp 2.6 No project yet

Vehicle comparison

"I want to compare vehicles"
Overlaps with favorites/wishlist (Opp 1.6); comparison may live on top of a more accessible favorites surface. Flag from product standup 2026-05-07 (Hanna).
Opp 2.7 No project yet

VDP wall of content

"The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me"
Tabbed listing-details block: scan plus drill. Hard constraint: all tab content stays crawlable (server-rendered, CSS-hidden). Local scoping in hop-os/projects/vdp-tabs. No Linear project yet.
VDP inspiration: QuirkyCampers
Branch 3

Booking flow

ATC → FBRS
First-timer 17% → ~18%; overall 25% → ~26%
3a. Empty / low-value steps
Opp 3a.1

Steps render with nothing to choose

"I'm being asked things that don't apply to me"
  • Skip addons and insurance when possible: behind skip_empty_booking_steps flag; realistic +0.4pp ATC→FBRS
  • Buy addons after booking: move add-on selection to BDP, keep the GMV
Opp 3a.2

27% drop on the first step

"The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged"
  • A/B test: insurance step before addons: swap order; measure step CR plus overall CR
3b. Sign-up / login (worst single dropoff)
Opp 3b.1

Login interrupts the booking

"Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking"
  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step: modal overlay, CTA fix, Google-auth bug fix
  • Offer registration with Phone number: lower-friction alternative
Step converts 68% globally; DACH+INT 65% vs Nordics 78%. Goal: all markets to at least 78%.
3c. About-you step (Nordic black hole)
Opp 3c.1

eID gate before owner acceptance

"Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive"
  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido: target +2pp About-you→FBRS Nordic; flag nordic_eid_after_booking
44% of SE about-you sessions never start eID; once started, ~89% complete. The block is cognitive, not technical.
Opp 3c.2

Duplicate and misdirected driver data

"I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)"
  • Improve about-you step: container for the Nordic 34% drop
  • Separate between main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile: confirm-or-edit pattern
  • Make the Driver's license question clearer
Opp 3c.3

Missing residence countries

"My country isn't in the list"
  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters: adds 12 EU countries; goal 250 confirmed bookings in 2026
3d. Payment step
Opp 3d.1

Payment options hidden below the fold

"I can't see all my payment options"
  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opp 3d.2

Unexplained payment failures

"Payment fails and I don't know why"
  • Payment step: many users struggle with the payment: explain plus prime
Opp 3d.3

Cancellation anxiety at commit

"The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit"
  • Timeline for booking in BDP: post-booking reassurance, deferred until VDP timeline ships; impact on this KR is indirect
Best treated upstream via Opp 2.1 (VDP timeline), which sets expectations early.
3e. Catch-alls and infrastructure
Opp 3e

Small fixes and experiment foundation

  • Misc Booking flow improvements: e.g. MC-1563 mandatory-field markers, MC-1565 double error on birthday
  • Feature flags: foundation for clean A/B reads on every project above

Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  • What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. Needs a Stef conversation; no project by design.
  • What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be the prerequisite to every first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out-of-scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP: rejected. Would push toward a classified model; trust should come from supply quality plus instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion: post-booking only, users already successful.
