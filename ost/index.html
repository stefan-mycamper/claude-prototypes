Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but our funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Investing in event tracking might be the prerequisite to every other first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out of scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected. Would push us toward a classified model; we want trust through supply quality and instant book, not message first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only, users already successful.
