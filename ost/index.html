Opportunity Solution Tree

Session → FBRS = 0.52%

Owner: Nils Alm  ·  Target date: 2026-06-30  ·  Product Team KR, Q2 2026  ·  Status: Active (at risk)
Outcome

Lift session to first-booking-request-sent conversion from ~0.43% to 0.52% by 2026-06-30. The funnel decomposes into three multiplicative gaps; those gaps are the three branches of this tree.

Sessions → VDP (~48%)  ×  VDP → ATC (~5%)  ×  ATC → FBRS (~17% first-timer / ~25% overall)  ≈  ~0.5%

Vehicle discovery

Session → VDP

Opp 1.2

Pickup location matters more than the list shows

"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"

Opp 1.3

Branch 2

Vehicle selection

VDP → ATC
+1 to 2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)

Opp 2.1

Booking is a leap into the unknown

"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"

  • Experiment: A/B 50/50 on VDP, 4 weeks; primary VDP → ATC
Opp 2.2

No social proof to reduce hesitation

"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"

  • Social proof: click percentiles, "recently booked", calendar freshness
Opp 2.3

Total price invisible on mobile

"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"

  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile: sticky footer with total price plus cost overlay
Opp 2.4Biggest lever

Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. Needs a Stef conversation.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall; funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be the prerequisite to every first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out-of-scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP: rejected. Would push toward a classified model; we want trust through supply quality plus instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion: post-booking only, users already successful.
