Opportunity Solution Tree

Session → FBRS = 0.52%

Owner: Nils Alm  ·  Target: 2026-06-30  ·  KR: Product Team, Q2 2026
Outcome
Lift session to first-booking-request-sent from ~0.43% to 0.52%
Across Q2 2026. Decomposed into three multiplicative funnel gaps below.
Sessions → VDP (~48%)  ×  VDP → ATC (~5%)  ×  ATC → FBRS (~17% first-timer / ~25% overall)  =  ~0.5%
Branch 1, Vehicle discovery Branch 2, Vehicle selection Branch 3, Booking flow No project yet Biggest project-less lever
Branch 1
Vehicle discovery
Session → VDP
Sustain ~48%, lift VDP→ATC indirectly via better intent
Opportunity 1.1
"I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need"
Renters can't filter on add-ons, equipment, or rules, so they enter the booking flow just to inspect. This pollutes ATC and burns user effort.
  • Search filters
Opportunity 1.2
"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"
Inspired by Yescapa; pickup location may be a stronger filter than we treat it as.
  • Search map
Opportunity 1.3
"The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out"
Listings blur together; nothing communicates that vehicles are real, distinctive, and currently active.
  • Vehicle personalisation / name
Opportunity 1.4 No project yet
"My request got declined and now I have to start over"
After a decline, the email and in-product fallback both send the renter into a generic search with no preserved context. Every decline that doesn't loop back into a continued search is a foregone subsequent FBRS.
Triggered by Nils experiencing this firsthand 2026-05-03. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.5 and 2.4. Candidate for triage.
Opportunity 1.5 No project yet
"I came back to keep looking and lost my place"
Returning renters restart from an empty search; guest state isn't persisted. We're throwing away the highest-intent cohort we have.
Likely a meaningful slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions converting at 1.38%. Candidate for triage.
Opportunity 1.6 No project yet
"I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked"
The current favorites/wishlist functionality seems hidden and not very accessible.
Opportunity 1.7 No project yet
"I'm planning different trips and I want to save vehicles to different favorite lists to keep my search focused"
Vehicles differ in availability and fit for the trip and are found at different locations, so different trips require different favorite lists.
Branch 2
Vehicle selection
VDP → ATC
+1 to 2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)
Opportunity 2.1
"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"
First-timers convert at 17% vs 24% overall. The VDP sells the vehicle but says nothing about the P2P process; clicking "Start booking" is a leap into the unknown.
  • Timeline for booking on VDP
Experiment: A/B test 50/50 on VDP, 4 weeks. Primary: VDP→ATC; secondary: first-time CR.
Opportunity 2.2
"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"
Reviews are flat (single star, no context). Renters lack social proof to reduce hesitation.
  • Rich reviews
  • Social proof
Opportunity 2.3
"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"
Mobile users can't see cost overview unless they ATC. The button asks too much for too little visible value.
  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile
Opportunity 2.4 Biggest lever
"This vehicle isn't quite it, and I don't see anywhere to go from here"
~102k sessions in 2 months land directly on a vehicle that doesn't match, then bounce. The user already invested a click to reach this page; sending them off into a generic search isn't acting where they are.
  • Vehicle recommendations on VDP
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
  • AI assistant to suggest three options, gathering feedback
Sizing: 50 to 140% of the KR gap on its own. Most direct way to execute the "shift 1-VDP into 2 to 3 VDP behaviour" thesis (+65 to +180 incremental FBRS/month).
Opportunity 2.5 No project yet
"Is this vehicle the right one?"
Add more information: bedding setup, clearer add-ons, better gallery without scrolling each image individually, gas consumption and carbon footprint.
Opportunity 2.6 No project yet
"I want to compare vehicles"
Overlap with favorites/wishlist (Opp 1.6); comparison may live as a feature on top of a more accessible favorites surface.
Flag from product standup 2026-05-07 (Hanna).
Opportunity 2.7 No project yet
"The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me"
The middle "Listing details" block takes a lot of vertical space and pushes trust signals and the booking CTA way down the page. Tabbed sections: Vehicle data, Equipment, Add-on Equipment, Add-on services, Rental Terms, Rental costs, Cancellation.
Hard constraint: all tab content must remain crawler-accessible (server-rendered HTML, hidden via CSS). Local scoping: hop-os/projects/vdp-tabs/opportunity.md.
Branch 3
Booking flow
ATC → FBRS
First-timer 17% → ~18%; overall 25% → ~26%
3a. Empty / low-value steps
Opportunity 3a.1
"I'm being asked things that don't apply to me"
Customize and Insurance steps render even when there's nothing to choose.
  • Skip addons and insurance when possible
  • Buy addons after booking
Behind skip_empty_booking_steps flag. Expected ceiling +0.8pp ATC→FBRS, realistic +0.4pp.
Opportunity 3a.2
"The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged"
27% drop on the first step. Hypothesis: it's the position, not the content.
  • A/B test: insurance step before addons
3b. Sign-up / login step
Opportunity 3b.1
"Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking"
Step converts at 68% globally; DACH+INT 65% vs Nordics 78%. Inline sign-up replaces the booking context.
  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step
  • Offer registration with Phone number
Experiment: close 13pp Nordic vs DACH gap; lift all markets to ≥78%.
3c. About-you step
Opportunity 3c.1
"Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive"
Nordic eID is a pre-booking gate. 44% of SE about-you sessions never start eID; once started, ~89% complete. The block is cognitive, not technical.
  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido
Target +2pp About-you → FBRS Nordic. Flag nordic_eid_after_booking, 4 to 6 weeks.
Opportunity 3c.2
"I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)"
Renters are confused by main-driver vs account-holder, and re-fill the same data on every booking attempt.
  • Improve about-you step
  • Separate between main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile
  • Make the Driver's license question clearer
Opportunity 3c.3
"My country isn't in the list"
Renters from 12 EU countries can't select their country of residence. ~50% drop on About-you for international segment.
  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters
Goal: 250 confirmed bookings in 2026 from new countries.
3d. Payment step
Opportunity 3d.1
"I can't see all my payment options"
"Pay later" is below the fold. Many users don't notice the option that would have unblocked them.
  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opportunity 3d.2
"Payment fails and I don't know why"
3D Secure, payment limits, online-purchase locks; we can't know which, but we can prime users.
  • Payment step, many users struggle with the payment
Opportunity 3d.3
"The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit"
Showing a near-closed free-cancellation window creates last-second anxiety (Joanna user test). Best treated upstream via Opp 2.1.
  • Timeline for booking in BDP
Post-booking, indirect KR impact. Deferred to after VDP timeline ships.

Infrastructure
Booking-flow plumbing

  • Misc Booking flow improvements

Strategic questions, not yet projects

Need alignment before they become product work. Both touch the booking flow but could reshape branches 2 and 3 entirely.

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. No project yet by design.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but our funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Investing in event tracking might be the prerequisite to every other first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out of scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected. Would push us toward a classified model; we want trust through supply quality + instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only, users already successful.
