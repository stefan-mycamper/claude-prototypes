Opportunity Solution Tree

Session → FBRS = 0.52%

Owner: Nils Alm  ·  Target date: 2026-06-30  ·  KR: Product Team KR, Q2 2026  ·  Health: At risk

Outcome

Lift session to first-booking-request-sent from ~0.43% to 0.52%

Across Q2 2026. Three multiplicative funnel gaps map to the three branches below.

Sessions → VDP (~48%)  ×  VDP → ATC (~5%)  ×  ATC → FBRS (~17% first-timer / ~25% overall)  ≈  ~0.5%

Branch 1

Vehicle discovery

Session → VDP (~48%)

Target: sustain ~48%; lift VDP→ATC indirectly via better intent

Opp 1.1

"I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need"

Renters can't filter on add-ons, equipment, or rules, so they enter the booking flow just to inspect.

  • Search filters
  • MC-1567 — default sort = distance
Opp 1.2

"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"

Inspired by Yescapa: maybe pickup location is a stronger filter than we treat it as.

  • Search map
Opp 1.3

"The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out"

Listings blur together; nothing communicates that vehicles are real, distinctive, and currently active.

  • Vehicle personalisation / name
Opp 1.4 No project yet

"My request got declined and now I have to start over"

Post-decline, the renter is dropped into a generic search with no preserved context. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.5 and 2.4.

Opp 1.5 No project yet

"I came back to keep looking and lost my place"

Returning renters restart from an empty search; guest and logged-in state isn't persisted. Likely a slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions.

Opp 1.6 No project yet

"I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked"

Favorites/wishlist is hidden and not very accessible; making it prominent could help.

Opp 1.7 No project yet

"I'm planning different trips and I want to save vehicles to different favorite lists to keep my search focused"

Different trips need different favorite lists, by availability, fit, and location.

Branch 2

Vehicle selection

VDP → ATC (~5%)

Target: +1 to 2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)

Opp 2.1

"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"

First-timers convert at 17% vs 24% overall. The VDP sells the vehicle but says nothing about the P2P process.

  • Timeline for booking on VDP
Opp 2.2

"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"

Reviews are flat (single star, no context); renters lack social proof to reduce hesitation.

  • Rich reviews
  • Social proof
Opp 2.3

"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"

Mobile users can't see cost overview unless they ATC; the button asks too much for too little visible value.

  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile
Opp 2.4 Biggest lever

"This vehicle isn't quite it, and I don't see anywhere to go from here"

~102k sessions in 2 months land on a non-matching vehicle and bounce. Turn the VDP into a path to a fitting vehicle. Sized at 50 to 140% of the KR gap on its own.

  • Vehicle recommendations on VDP
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
  • AI assistant to suggest three options, getting feedback
Opp 2.5 No project yet

"Is this vehicle the right one?"

Add more info: bedding setup, clearer add-ons, a better gallery, gas consumption and carbon footprint.

Opp 2.6 No project yet

"I want to compare vehicles"

Overlaps favorites/wishlist (Opp 1.6); comparison may live on top of a more accessible favorites surface. Flag from standup 2026-05-07 (Hanna).

Opp 2.7 No project yet

"The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me"

Tabbed sections for a scannable fit-check with on-demand depth. Hard constraint: all tab content stays crawlable (server-rendered, Schema.org preserved). Local scoping in hop-os/projects/vdp-tabs/.

Branch 3

Booking flow

ATC → FBRS (~17% / ~25%)

Target: first-timer 17% → ~18%; overall 25% → ~26%

3a · Empty / low-value steps

Opp 3a.1

"I'm being asked things that don't apply to me"

Customize and Insurance steps render even when there's nothing to choose.

  • Skip addons and insurance when possible
  • Buy addons after booking
Opp 3a.2

"The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged"

27% drop on the first step. Hypothesis: it's the position, not the content.

  • A/B test: insurance step before addons

3b · Sign-up / login step

Opp 3b.1

"Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking"

Inline sign-up replaces booking context. Step converts 68% globally; DACH+INT 65% vs Nordics 78%.

  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step
  • Offer registration with Phone number

3c · About-you step

Opp 3c.1

"Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive"

Nordic eID is a pre-booking gate. 44% of SE about-you sessions never start eID; once started ~89% complete. The block is cognitive, not technical.

  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido
Opp 3c.2

"I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)"

Renters are confused by main-driver vs account-holder, and re-fill the same data on every attempt.

  • Improve about-you step
  • Separate between main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile
  • Make the Driver's license question clearer
Opp 3c.3

"My country isn't in the list"

Renters from 12 EU countries can't select residence; ~50% drop on About-you for the international segment.

  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters

3d · Payment step

Opp 3d.1

"I can't see all my payment options"

"Pay later" is below the fold; many users don't notice the option that would have unblocked them.

  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opp 3d.2

"Payment fails and I don't know why"

3D Secure, payment limits, online-purchase locks; we can't know which, but we can prime users.

  • Payment step — many users struggle with the payment
Opp 3d.3

"The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit"

A near-closed free-cancellation window creates last-second anxiety. Best treated upstream via 2.1. BDP timeline is post-booking, so impact is indirect.

  • Timeline for booking in BDP

3e · Catch-alls and infrastructure

Opp 3e

Small fixes and A/B foundations

  • Misc Booking flow improvements
  • Feature flags

Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? A binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. The highest-leverage first-timer lever, but it needs a Stef conversation first.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be the prerequisite to every other first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out-of-scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected; would push toward a classified model. We want trust through supply quality plus instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only; users are already successful.
