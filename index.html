Branch 1

Vehicle discovery

Funnel stage: Session → VDP
Target: sustain ~48%, lift VDP→ATC indirectly via better intent
Opportunity 1.1
"I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need"
Renters can't filter on add-ons, equipment, or rules, so they enter the booking flow just to inspect. Pollutes ATC, burns user effort.
  • Search filters, add-on/equipment/rules
Opportunity 1.2
"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"
Inspired by Yescapa, maybe pickup location is a stronger filter than we treat it as.
  • Search map, map-first results
Opportunity 1.3
"The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out"
Listings blur together; nothing communicates that vehicles are real, distinctive, and currently active.
  • Vehicle personalisation / name
Opportunity 1.4
No project yet
"My request got declined and now I have to start over"
Post-decline emails and in-product fallback drop renters into a generic search with no preserved context. Bad emotional moment, foregone subsequent FBRS. Shares intent-preservation problem with 1.5 and 2.4.
  • Candidate for triage
Opportunity 1.5
No project yet
"I came back to keep looking and lost my place"
Returning renters restart from an empty search. Guest state isn't persisted; even logged-in users start over. Likely meaningful slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions at 1.38% Session → ATC.
  • Candidate for triage
Opportunity 1.6
No project yet
"I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked"
Current favorites/wishlist functionality is hidden and not very accessible. Make it a more prominent surface.
  • Candidate for triage
Opportunity 1.7
No project yet
"I'm planning different trips and I want to save vehicles to different favorite lists to keep my search focused"
Vehicles differ in availability, fit, and location, so different trips require different favorite lists.
  • Candidate for triage
Branch 2

Vehicle selection

Funnel stage: VDP → ATC
Target: +1 to 2pp absolute, 5% to 6 to 7%
Opportunity 2.1
"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"
First-timers convert at 17% vs 24% overall. The VDP sells the vehicle but says nothing about the P2P process.
  • Timeline for booking on VDP
Opportunity 2.2
"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"
Reviews are flat (single star, no context). Renters lack social proof to reduce hesitation.
  • Rich reviews
  • Social proof
Opportunity 2.3
"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"
Mobile users can't see cost overview unless they ATC. The button asks too much for too little visible value.
  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile
Opportunity 2.4
Biggest lever
"This vehicle isn't quite it, and I don't see anywhere to go from here"
~102k sessions in 2 months land directly on a non-matching vehicle, then bounce. Sized at 50 to 140% of the KR gap. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.4 and 1.5.
  • Filter recommendations on VDP, vehicle recommendations
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
  • AI assistant to suggest three options getting feedback
Opportunity 2.5
No project yet
"Is this vehicle the right one?"
Add more information on bedding setup, add-ons, gallery (no scrolling each image), gas consumption, carbon footprint.
  • Candidate for triage
Opportunity 2.6
No project yet
"I want to compare vehicles"
Overlap with favorites/wishlist (Opp 1.6). Comparison may live as a feature on top of a more accessible favorites surface. Flag from product standup 2026-05-07 (Hanna).
  • Candidate for triage
Opportunity 2.7
No project yet
"The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me"
Middle "Listing details" block pushes trust signals and CTA down. Tabbed sections (Vehicle data, Equipment, Add-ons, etc.) with server-rendered content for SEO. Local scoping: hop-os/projects/vdp-tabs/opportunity.md.
  • Candidate for triage
Branch 3

Booking flow

Funnel stage: ATC → FBRS
Target: first-timer 17% to ~18%, overall 25% to ~26%
3a. Empty / low-value steps
Opportunity 3a.1
"I'm being asked things that don't apply to me"
Customize and Insurance steps render even when there's nothing to choose.
  • Skip addons and insurance when possible
  • Buy addons after booking
Opportunity 3a.2
"The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged"
27% drop on the first step. Hypothesis: it's the position, not the content.
  • A/B test, insurance step before addons
3b. Sign-up / login step, worst single dropoff
Opportunity 3b.1
"Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking"
Inline sign-up step replaces booking context. Step converts at 68% globally; DACH+INT 65% vs Nordics 78%.
  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step
  • Offer registration with phone number
3c. About-you step, Nordic black hole
Opportunity 3c.1
"Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive"
Nordic eID is a pre-booking gate. 44% of SE about-you sessions never start eID. Block is cognitive, not technical.
  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido
Opportunity 3c.2
"I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)"
Renters are confused by main-driver vs account-holder, and re-fill the same data every booking.
  • Improve about-you step
  • Separate main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile
  • Make the driver's license question clearer
Opportunity 3c.3
"My country isn't in the list"
Renters from 12 EU countries can't select country of residence. ~50% drop on About-you for international segment.
  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters
3d. Payment step
Opportunity 3d.1
"I can't see all my payment options"
"Pay later" is below the fold. Users miss the option that would have unblocked them.
  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opportunity 3d.2
"Payment fails and I don't know why"
3D Secure, payment limits, online-purchase locks. We can't know which, but we can prime users.
  • Payment step, many users struggle with the payment
Opportunity 3d.3
"The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit"
Best treated upstream via Opp 2.1 (VDP timeline). BDP timeline is post-booking; impact on this KR is indirect.
  • Timeline for booking in BDP, post-booking
3e. Catch-alls and infrastructure
Opportunity 3e.1
Misc fixes and the experimentation foundation
Small fixes that don't warrant their own opportunity, plus the feature-flag foundation for clean A/B reads.
  • Misc booking flow improvements
  • Feature flags

Strategic questions, not yet projects

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. Highest-leverage first-timer lever, needs a Stef conversation.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Investing in event tracking may be the prerequisite to every other first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out-of-scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected. Would push us toward a classified model; we want trust through supply quality plus instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only, users already successful.
