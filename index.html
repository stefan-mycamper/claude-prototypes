Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. Needs a Stef conversation before it becomes product work.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be a prerequisite to every first-timer experiment.

  • BDP additional driver display confusion — post-booking only; users already successful.
  • Build a one-page checkout — bigger structural rework; out of scope for Q2 unless incremental step-skips don't add up.
  • Prominently place a phone number in the booking flow — no project yet; candidate for triage.
