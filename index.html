1Vehicle discovery
Funnel stage: Session → VDP
Target: sustain ~48%; lift VDP → ATC indirectly via better intent
Opp 1.1
"I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need"
Renters can't filter on add-ons, equipment, or rules, so they enter the booking flow just to inspect.
  • Search filters
Opp 1.2
"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"
Inspired by Yescapa: pickup location may be a stronger filter than we treat it as.
  • Search map
Opp 1.3
"The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out"
Listings blur together; nothing communicates that vehicles are real, distinctive, currently active.
  • Vehicle personalisation / name
Opp 1.4 No project yet
"My request got declined and now I have to start over"
Decline emails and the in-product fallback drop renters into a generic search with no context preserved. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.5 and 2.4.
Opp 1.5 No project yet
"I came back to keep looking and lost my place"
Returning renters restart from an empty search. Likely a meaningful slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions converting at 1.38%.
Opp 1.6 No project yet
"I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked"
Favorites/wishlist functionality is hidden and not accessible. Make it a more prominent surface.
Opp 1.7 No project yet
"I'm planning different trips and want to save vehicles to different favorite lists"
Different trips need different favorite lists; vehicles differ in availability, fit, and location.
2Vehicle selection
Funnel stage: VDP → ATC
Target: +1 to +2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)
Opp 2.1
"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"
First-timers convert at 17% vs 24% overall. The VDP sells the vehicle but says nothing about the P2P process.
  • Timeline for booking on VDP
Opp 2.2
"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"
Reviews are flat (single star, no context). Renters lack social proof to reduce hesitation.
  • Rich reviews
  • Social proof
Opp 2.3
"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"
Mobile users can't see cost overview unless they ATC. The button asks too much for too little visible value.
  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile
Opp 2.4 Biggest lever
"This vehicle isn't quite it, and I don't see anywhere to go from here"
~102k sessions in 2 months land directly on a vehicle that doesn't match, then bounce. Sized at 50 to 140% of the KR gap. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.4 and 1.5.
  • Vehicle recommendations on VDP
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
Opp 2.5 No project yet
"Is this vehicle the right one?"
Add information on bedding setup, clearer add-ons, better gallery, gas/CO2 footprint.
Opp 2.6 No project yet
"I want to compare vehicles"
Overlap with favorites/wishlist (Opp 1.6); comparison may live as a feature on top of a more accessible favorites surface. Flag from product standup 2026-05-07 (Hanna).
Opp 2.7 No project yet
"The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me"
Listing details block pushes trust signals and CTA way down. Tabs allow scan + drill while preserving SEO via server-rendered DOM. Local scoping in hop-os/projects/vdp-tabs/opportunity.md.
3Booking flow
Funnel stage: ATC → FBRS
Target: first-timer 17% → ~18%; overall 25% → ~26%
3a · Empty / low-value steps
Opp 3a.1
"I'm being asked things that don't apply to me"
Customize and Insurance steps render even when there's nothing to choose.
  • Skip addons and insurance when possible
  • Buy addons after booking
Opp 3a.2
"The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged"
27% drop on the first step. Hypothesis: position, not content.
  • A/B test: insurance step before addons
3b · Sign-up / login (worst single dropoff)
Opp 3b.1
"Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking"
Inline sign-up replaces the booking context. Step CR 68% globally; DACH+INT 65% vs Nordics 78%.
  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step
  • Offer registration with Phone number
3c · About-you (Nordic black hole)
Opp 3c.1
"Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive"
Nordic eID is a pre-booking gate. 44% of SE about-you sessions never start eID; once started, ~89% complete. The block is cognitive, not technical.
  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido
Opp 3c.2
"I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)"
Renters confused by main-driver vs account-holder, and re-fill the same data on every booking.
  • Improve about-you step
  • Separate between main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile
  • Make the Driver's license question clearer
Opp 3c.3
"My country isn't in the list"
Renters from 12 EU countries can't select country of residence; ~50% drop on About-you for international segment.
  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters
3d · Payment
Opp 3d.1
"I can't see all my payment options"
"Pay later" is below the fold. Many users don't notice the option that would have unblocked them.
  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opp 3d.2
"Payment fails and I don't know why"
3D Secure, payment limits, online-purchase locks. Prime users for what's coming.
  • Payment step, many users struggle
Opp 3d.3
"The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit"
Best treated upstream via Opp 2.1 (VDP timeline). BDP timeline is post-FBRS; impact on this KR is indirect.
  • Timeline for booking in BDP
3e · Catch-alls and infrastructure
Opp 3e
Misc fixes and platform foundations
Bucket for small fixes (mandatory-fields markers, double error on birthday) and the foundation for clean A/B reads.
  • Misc Booking flow improvements
  • Feature flags

Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  • What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning.
  • What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be the prerequisite to every other first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out of scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected; would push us toward a classified model. We want trust through supply quality plus instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only; users already successful.
