Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  1. What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a request is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Highest-leverage first-timer lever; needs a Stef conversation before becoming product work.
  2. What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall; funnel events don't yet distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be the prerequisite to every other first-timer experiment.

Explicitly killed / out-of-scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected; would push us toward a classified model. We want trust via supply quality and instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only; users already successful.
