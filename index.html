Branch 1

Vehicle discovery

Funnel stage: Session → VDP
Target: sustain ~48%; lift VDP → ATC via better intent
Opp 1.1
Filtering by what I need
"I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need"
  • Search filters, add-on, equipment, rules filters
  • Issue MC-1567, default sort = distance for location-based searches
Opp 1.2
Pickup location as a discovery signal
"Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list"
  • Search map, map-first results
Opp 1.3
Listings that stand out
"The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out"
  • Vehicle personalisation / name, owner-named vehicles for character + marketplace-vitality loop
Opp 1.4 No project yet
Recover from a declined request
"My request got declined and now I have to start over"
  • Candidate for triage. Shares the intent-preservation problem with 1.5 and 2.4.
Opp 1.5 No project yet
Resume an earlier search
"I came back to keep looking and lost my place"
  • Candidate for triage. Likely a slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions at 1.38%.
Opp 1.6 No project yet
Find vehicles I liked earlier
"I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked"
  • Favorites/wishlist is hidden today; make it a more prominent surface.
Opp 1.7 No project yet
Multiple favorite lists per trip
"I'm planning different trips and want to save vehicles to different favorite lists"
  • Different trips need different shortlists; a single list isn't enough.
Branch 2

Vehicle selection

Funnel stage: VDP → ATC
Target: +1 to 2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)
Opp 2.1
Make the booking journey legible on the VDP
"I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit"
  • Timeline for booking on VDP, surfaces the 6-step booking journey
  • A/B test 50/50, 4 weeks. Primary: VDP → ATC; secondary: first-time CR.
Opp 2.2
Trust signals for the owner and the vehicle
"I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle"
  • Rich reviews, renter / trip context, sub-ratings, photos
  • Social proof, click-percentile, "recently booked", calendar-updated
Opp 2.3
Total price visible without committing
"I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow"
  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile, sticky footer + cost overlay
Opp 2.4 Biggest lever
Turn the VDP into a path to a fitting vehicle
"This vehicle isn't quite it and I don't see anywhere to go from here"
  • Filter recommendations on VDP
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
  • AI assistant to suggest three options, getting feedback
  • Sizing: +65 to +180 incremental FBRS/month, 50 to 140% of the KR gap.
Opp 2.5 No project yet
Is this vehicle the right one?
"Is this vehicle the right one?"
  • Bedding setup; clearer add-ons; gallery without per-image scroll; gas / CO2 info.
Opp 2.6 No project yet
Compare vehicles
"I want to compare vehicles"
  • Overlaps with Opp 1.6; likely a feature on top of a better favorites surface.
Opp 2.7 No project yet
Tab the VDP middle section
"The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me"
  • Tabs: vehicle data, equipment, add-on equipment, add-on services, terms, costs, cancellation.
  • Hard constraint: SEO preserved (server-rendered, schema.org markup intact).
Branch 3

Booking flow

Funnel stage: ATC → FBRS
Target: first-timer 17% → ~18%; overall 25% → ~26%
3a · Empty / low-value steps
Opp 3a.1
Skip steps that don't apply
"I'm being asked things that don't apply to me"
  • Skip addons and insurance when possible, flag-gated; ceiling +0.8pp ATC → FBRS
  • Buy addons after booking, move add-ons to BDP
Opp 3a.2
First-step shock
"The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged"
  • A/B test: insurance step before addons, test position vs. content
3b · Sign-up / login
Opp 3b.1
Sign-up that doesn't break flow
"Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking"
  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step
  • Offer registration with phone number
  • Close the 13pp Nordic / DACH gap; lift all markets to ≥ 78%.
3c · About-you (Nordic black hole)
Opp 3c.1
Move Nordic eID after booking
"Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive"
  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido
  • PostHog flag nordic_eid_after_booking, 4 to 6 weeks. Target +2pp.
Opp 3c.2
Sort out main driver vs account holder
"I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)"
  • Improve about-you step, container for the Nordic 34% drop
  • Separate main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile, confirm-or-edit for repeat senders
  • Make driver's license question clearer
Opp 3c.3
Add the missing EU countries
"My country isn't in the list"
  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters, goal: 250 confirmed bookings in 2026
3d · Payment
Opp 3d.1
Surface all payment options
"I can't see all my payment options"
  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opp 3d.2
Prime users for payment failures
"Payment fails and I don't know why"
  • Payment step: many users struggle with payment, explain + prime
Opp 3d.3
Defuse last-second cancellation anxiety
"The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit"
  • Timeline for booking in BDP, post-FBRS; indirect KR impact
  • Best treated upstream via Opp 2.1 (VDP timeline).
3e · Catch-alls and infrastructure
Opp 3e
Plumbing for the rest of Branch 3
"Lots of small fixes; clean A/B reads need feature flags"
  • Misc booking flow improvements, e.g. MC-1563, MC-1565
  • Feature flags, foundation for clean A/B reads

Strategic questions (not yet projects)

  • What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding-as-request is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops, owner experience, positioning. Needs a Stef conversation.
  • What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking may be the prerequisite to every first-timer experiment.

Killed / out of scope

  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP, rejected. Pushes toward a classifieds model; we want trust via supply quality + instant-book.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion, post-booking; users already successful.
  • One-page check-out, bigger structural rework; out of scope for Q2 unless incremental step-skips don't add up.
