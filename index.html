1 Branch

Vehicle discovery

Session → VDP · Sustain ~48%; lift VDP→ATC indirectly via better intent
Opp 1.1
I can't tell from search whether a vehicle has the things I need

Renters can't filter on add-ons, equipment, or rules, so they enter the booking flow just to inspect. Pollutes ATC and burns user effort.

  • Search filters
Opp 1.2
Where vehicles are picked up matters more than I can see in the list

Inspired by Yescapa: maybe pickup location is a stronger filter than we treat it as.

  • Search map
Opp 1.3
The marketplace feels generic, listings don't stand out

Listings blur together; nothing communicates that vehicles are real, distinctive, and currently active.

  • Vehicle personalisation / name
Opp 1.4
No project yet
My request got declined and now I have to start over

Post-decline emails and in-product fallbacks send the renter into a generic search with no preserved context. Most renters need multiple requests; every decline that doesn't loop back is a foregone subsequent FBRS.

Opp 1.5
No project yet
I came back to keep looking and lost my place

Returning renters restart from an empty search. Guest state isn't persisted; even logged-in users start over. Likely a meaningful slice of the 169k single-VDP sessions at 1.38% Session→ATC.

Opp 1.6
No project yet
I sent an unsuccessful booking request and came back and can't find the other vehicles I liked

The current favorites/wishlist functionality is hidden and not very accessible. Make it a more prominent part of the experience.

Opp 1.7
No project yet
I'm planning different trips and want to save vehicles to different favorite lists

Vehicles differ in availability and fit; different trips happen at different locations, so different trips require different favorite lists.

2 Branch

Vehicle selection

VDP → ATC · +1 to 2pp absolute (5% → 6 to 7%)
Opp 2.1
I don't know what booking actually involves before I commit

First-timers convert at 17% vs 24% overall. The VDP sells the vehicle but says nothing about the P2P process. Clicking "Start booking" is a leap into the unknown.

  • Timeline for booking on VDP
Opp 2.2
I can't tell if other people trust this owner / vehicle

Reviews are flat (single star, no context). Renters lack social proof to reduce hesitation.

  • Rich reviews
  • Social proof
Opp 2.3
I can't see total price on mobile without entering the booking flow

Mobile users can't see cost overview unless they ATC. The button asks too much for too little visible value.

  • Unify ATC button on desktop and mobile
Opp 2.4
Biggest lever
This vehicle isn't quite it, and I don't see anywhere to go from here

~102k sessions land on a non-matching VDP and bounce. Turn non-fitting landings into a path to fitting ones without making the user start over. Sized at 50 to 140% of the KR gap.

  • Filter recommendations on VDP
  • Arrow navigation to next VDP
  • AI assistant to suggest three options getting feedback
Opp 2.5
No project yet
Is this vehicle the right one?

More info: bedding setup, clearer add-ons, better gallery (no per-image scrolling), gas consumption and carbon footprint.

Opp 2.6
No project yet
I want to compare vehicles

Overlap with favorites/wishlist (Opp 1.6). Comparison may live as a feature on top of a more accessible favorites surface rather than a standalone view.

Opp 2.7
No project yet
The VDP is a wall of content and I get tired before I reach the signals that convert me

Listing-details block buries trust signals and the CTA. Tabbed sections (Vehicle data, Equipment, Add-ons, Terms, Costs, Cancellation) with all content server-rendered for SEO. Local scoping in hop-os/projects/vdp-tabs.

3 Branch

Booking flow

ATC → FBRS · First-timer 17% → ~18%; overall 25% → ~26%
3a · Empty / low-value steps
Opp 3a.1
I'm being asked things that don't apply to me

Customize and Insurance steps render even when there's nothing to choose.

  • Skip addons and insurance when possible
  • Buy addons after booking
Opp 3a.2
The first step of the flow kills me before I've engaged

27% drop on the first step. Hypothesis: position, not content.

  • A/B test: insurance step before addons
3b · Sign-up / login step
Opp 3b.1
Login feels like a separate task that interrupted my booking

Inline sign-up replaces booking context. Step converts at 68% globally; DACH+INT 65% vs Nordics 78%.

  • Sign-up/login as overlay instead of inline step
  • Offer registration with phone number
3c · About-you step
Opp 3c.1
Bank ID before I even know if the owner accepts feels excessive

Nordic eID is a pre-booking gate. 44% of SE about-you sessions never start eID. Once started, ~89% complete. The block is cognitive, not technical.

  • Move Nordic eID verification to after booking like Onfido
Opp 3c.2
I'm filling out the same person twice (or for the wrong person)

Renters are confused by main-driver vs account-holder, and re-fill the same data on every booking attempt.

  • Improve about-you step
  • Separate between main driver and account on about-you
  • Main driver details and additional driver
  • Save DL to profile
  • Make the Driver's license question clearer
Opp 3c.3
My country isn't in the list

Renters from 12 EU countries can't select their country of residence. ~50% drop on About-you for international segment.

  • Allowlist all EU countries for renters
3d · Payment step
Opp 3d.1
I can't see all my payment options

"Pay later" is below the fold. Many users don't notice the option that would have unblocked them.

  • Show all payment options above the fold
Opp 3d.2
Payment fails and I don't know why

3D Secure, payment limits, online-purchase locks. We can't know which, but we can prime users.

  • Payment step, many users struggle with the payment
Opp 3d.3
The cancellation policy makes me anxious right at commit

Near-closed free-cancellation window creates last-second anxiety. Best treated upstream via VDP timeline (Opp 2.1). BDP timeline is post-booking, listed for completeness.

  • Timeline for booking in BDP
3e · Catch-alls and infrastructure
Opp 3e.1
Small fixes and platform foundations

Bucket for small fixes (mandatory-fields markers, double error on birthday) and the feature-flag foundation needed for clean A/B reads.

  • Misc Booking flow improvements
  • Feature flags
Strategic questions (not yet projects)
  • What should "Buchungsanfrage" actually be? Binding booking framed as a "request" is a likely root cause of the 17% first-timer CR. Touches pricing, ops load, owner experience, competitive positioning. No project yet, by design.
  • What does the first-timer booking experience need to look like? First-timer 17% vs 24% overall, but funnel events don't distinguish first-timers per step. Event tracking is likely the prerequisite.
Explicitly killed / out-of-scope
  • Move "message owner" button next to ATC on VDP. Rejected. Would push toward a classified model; we want trust through supply quality and instant-book, not message-first.
  • BDP additional driver display confusion. Post-booking only; users already successful.
